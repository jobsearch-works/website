How It Works

Three intelligent layers working seamlessly together

Automation Engine

Prebuilt logic handles routine tasks instantly — autofilling forms, attaching resumes, and answering repeat questions with zero delay.

AI-Powered Agents

When automation encounters something new, LLMs reason through novel questions and generate customized documents using your knowledge base.

Human Oversight

As a last resort, our team reviews flagged issues, improves templates, and feeds better examples back — making the platform smarter over time.

Smart, Simple, Effortless

Tailored Resumes

Every application gets a customized resume that highlights the most relevant experience for each role.

Intelligent Answers

Our AI understands context and crafts thoughtful responses to application questions in your voice.

Complete Tracking
